Join Our Team! Work with Children with Special Needs at TriCounties Speech Services, Inc.

We’re always looking for talented and professional therapists to help fulfill a great need. Enjoy flexible hours, a fun and energetic work environment and the reward of making a difference in someone’s life.

Therapists at TriCounties Speech Services use the latest technology to help children learn and to keep parents informed about their child’s therapy results. You will receive ongoing hands-on training, in-house and online. We have a work/play culture in our office so that all employees enjoy their work and have fun!
